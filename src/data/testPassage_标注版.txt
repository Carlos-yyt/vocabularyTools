To begin with , taking art and music lessons may aggravate(使恶化) financial burdens on ordinary families .A recent survey conducted by a prestigious(有威望的) university shows that not only may such courses cost at least 10,000 yuan for each semester , but hiring artistic teachers is not a small overhead , which means that their parents have to save more money from other expenditures(支出) to afford this new cost .It is clear that in order to teach their children , parents have to pay for the costs on pigment(颜料) , cloth and other instruments .In fact , by saving the spending on those courses , parents can allocate more money to students ' majors like English or Mathematics .
